About

GogoLabs builds production-grade tools for creators who need speed and precision. Our apps are designed around real post workflows: less friction, local-first processing, and outputs you can actually ship.

Apps

Coupez

Coupez detects shot changes locally, lets you compare source vs target edits, and exports AAF-ready timelines for Pro Tools and other AAF-compatible DAWs.

  • Single-video cut detection with confidence controls.
  • Two-video compare flow with move/replace/delete mapping.
  • AAF export and conform plan workflows for post teams.

MastersChecker

MastersChecker combines loudness compliance, dialogue measurement, and FFmpeg-based finishing in one macOS app for faster delivery validation.

  • Batch loudness checks (Integrated, LRA, True Peak).
  • Preset-based dialogue targets (Netflix, Disney Plus 5.1, and custom).
  • PDF reporting plus export queue with pro video codecs.
