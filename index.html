Apps

Coupez

Coupez is designed for editors and post teams who need to move from picture analysis to conform decisions without wasting time. The app runs locally on macOS, detects shot changes with adjustable confidence, and helps you inspect every cut with frame-level context before export.

  • Single-video cut detection with threshold, interval, and shot-length controls.
  • Two-video compare workflow to identify replace, move, and delete events quickly.
  • AAF export for Pro Tools and other AAF-compatible DAWs.
  • Conform-plan generation and apply flow when Pro Tools workflows are needed.
  • Local-first pipeline with fallback analysis layers for reliable media handling.

MastersChecker

MastersChecker is built for mixers, post teams, and delivery managers who need reliable compliance decisions without bouncing between multiple tools. It brings loudness analysis, dialogue measurement, reporting, and finishing workflows into one focused macOS workspace, so validation and final outputs happen faster and with fewer manual steps.

  • Complete loudness checks: Integrated, LRA, True Peak, duration, and timing context.
  • Dialogue intelligence with delivery presets like Netflix and Disney Plus 5.1.
  • Preset-driven consistency checks across full batches before final delivery.
  • Built-in PDF reporting for clear client and platform handoff documentation.
  • FFmpeg-based finishing queue with H.264, H.265, ProRes, DNxHD, and DNxHR options.
