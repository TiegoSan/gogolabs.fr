Support

Need help with a GogoLabs app? We are available for bug reports, account questions, feature requests, and technical support.

Contact

Email us at contact@gogolabs.fr. Please include your app name, device model, iOS/macOS version, and a short issue description.

Coupez Support

  • Describe the exact step where the issue appears.
  • Attach a screenshot or screen recording when possible.
  • Include the file format you are using (for example WAV, AIFF, MP3).

Response Time

We usually reply within 2 business days.
